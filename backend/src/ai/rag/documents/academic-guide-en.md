# Academic Guide - Faculty of Engineering, Menoufia University

## Credit Hour System
The faculty operates on a credit hour system where each course has specific credit hours.
One credit hour equals one theory hour, two practical hours, or three lab hours.
The normal academic load is 15-18 credit hours per semester.

## Course Registration
Registration is done electronically through the student portal.
Dates are set by Student Affairs at the beginning of each semester.
Students must consult their academic advisor before registration.
Students may register for higher-level courses with advisor approval.

## Academic Advising
Each student is assigned an academic advisor from the faculty.
Students meet with their advisor at least once per semester.
The academic advisor helps students select appropriate courses.
The advisor monitors the student's academic progress.

## Transfer Between Specializations
Students may apply for transfer between specializations after the preparatory year.
A minimum CGPA of 2.5 is required for transfer.
Transfers are based on available seats and internal coordination.

## Examinations and Study
Midterm exams are held in the 7th or 8th week of study.
Final exams are held in weeks 15-16 of study.
Minimum lecture attendance is 75% to be eligible for exams.
