# Faculty of Engineering - Menoufia University Regulations

## Chapter 1: General Provisions
These internal regulations govern study, examinations, and graduation at the Faculty of Engineering, Menoufia University.
The duration of study is five academic years to obtain a Bachelor's degree in Engineering.
Study begins with a preparatory year.

## Chapter 2: Study System
Semester system: Fall semester, Spring semester, and optional Summer semester.
Maximum credit hours per semester: 22 hours.
Minimum credit hours per semester: 12 hours.
Maximum credit hours for summer semester: 9 hours.

## Chapter 3: Registration
Course registration occurs in the first two weeks of the semester.
Student must have a minimum CGPA of 2.0 to register for 18 or more credit hours.
Students may add/drop courses during the first week of study.
Students may withdraw from a course until the eighth week.

## Chapter 4: Examinations and Evaluation
Examinations are held at the end of each semester.
Grade distribution: Coursework 30%, Midterm exam 20%, Final exam 50%.
Oral and practical examinations are conducted before the final exam.
Passing grade is 60% of the total marks.

## Chapter 5: Failure and Academic Warning
A student with a CGPA below 2.0 is placed on academic warning.
A warning is issued if the CGPA remains below 2.0 for two consecutive semesters.
If the warning persists for three consecutive semesters, the student is dismissed from the faculty.
Students may appeal exam results within 15 days of announcement.

## Chapter 6: Graduation
Graduation requires successful completion of all prescribed courses.
Minimum CGPA for graduation: 2.0.
The student is awarded a Bachelor's degree in Engineering in a specialization.
Overall grade classification: Excellent (3.5-4.0), Very Good (3.0-3.49), Good (2.5-2.99), Pass (2.0-2.49).
